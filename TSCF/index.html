Wawin National School of Excellence

Wawin National School of Excellence is one the prestigious National School of Excellences in the country. Wawin is located in Huon Gulf district Morobe province along the highlands highway. The station 40 mile is where students take a local truck to the school.
Wawin National School of Excellence is situated in a peaceful environment where everything is in tune with the nature. The environment makes studying more effective and productive.

Wawin TSCF

The TSCF is the major student ministry in Wawin National School of Excellence along with other student fellowships including the Catholic students, Lutheran Students, Baptist and Seventhday Sudent ministries. Wawin organises school crusades and other religios and school related activities in Wawin National High.

TSCF

The TSCF (Teasury Student Christian Fellowship) in Wawin National School of Excellence is the leading religious group that organizes all religious activities, including crusades, outreaches and fundrainsing activities in the school.The TSCF ministry is a students ministry that comprises of all the denominations gathering togather under a common belief shared through Jesus Christ. TSCF focuses on helping students grow spiritually and morally. This group's core mission is to foster a loving and respectful environment in the school that encourages students to develop their faith while also developing their character and studies.

One of the reasons why TSCF is highly regarded is because the group members exhibit a high level of commitment, dedication, and love for one another. These qualities emerge from the shared belief in the teachings of Jesus Christ; thus, promoting the importance of love, kindness, and service to others. The members always remain motivated to serve the fellow students, the school, and the local community selflessly. TSCF provides various opportunities for the students to engage in activities that help them grow spiritually. With regular prayer meetings on Wednesday nights and fellowship gatherings on Friday nights and Sunday morning main service, students can connect more deeply with their faith. The group also offers various avenues to help students grow their talents and skills, these activities range from fundraising nights, concert night and many more that foster leadership qualities.

The students who participate in TSCF regularly have shared exciting stories about their experiences throughout the journey. They talk about how they have grown not only spiritually but also on a personal level. They have shared how TSCF has helped them in numerous ways, such as developing their self-confidence, leadership skills, and decision-making abilities. The TSCF in Wawin National High School has proven to be a valuable and positive group that provides support and encouragement for its members. It has successfully facilitated a sense of community and helped students grow, both spiritually and personally. It is an essential part of the school and an excellent opportunity for anyone who wants to grow closer to God.
